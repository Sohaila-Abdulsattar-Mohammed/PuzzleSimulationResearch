How to play word search on this website

You will be provided with a word search grid, and a list of eight words to find. Observe the grid, and once you find a word, you can select it as per the instructions below.

If you are using a laptop/desktop, you select a word by clicking on its first letter with the mouse, then dragging the mouse in the direction of the rest of the word.

Once you release your click, if the word you selected is indeed correct, it will be highlighted and crossed off from the list.

If you are using a touch-screen device (phone, tablet, etc), you select a word by clicking on each of its letters.

To confirm your selection, click anywhere outside the word search grid (highlighted in red in this picture for your reference). If the word you selected is indeed correct, it will be highlighted and crossed off from the list.
To deselect a selection, also click outside the word search grid.

Once you are done, you will be automatically prompted to continue.


Please ensure that you select the letters of a word in order. For instance, in selecting the word "state" as above, start from the letter "s" all the way to "e", do not go in reverse order.


You will start off first with a practice round to get a feel of how to complete the task; then, you will move on to the official round. If you would like to, you may skip the practice round by clicking on the "Skip Practice Round" button you will find at the top right of the page.
